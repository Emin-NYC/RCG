About us

At Robin Consulting, we're dedicated to bridging the technology gap for seniors. Our mission is to provide engaging, understandable, and practical tech education.

We enable seniors to connect with loved ones, access important services, and explore the digital world confidently and safely.

Our Services

  • Using Smart Devices Safely
  • Protecting Personal Information
  • Social Media and Digital News Navigation

Interactive Workshops - From basics to advanced topics, learn in a supportive environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start with your seminars?

Starting is easy! Just register through our website or call us directly. We offer both online and in-person sessions depending on your preference.

What technology skills can I learn?

You can learn a variety of skills from basic computer use and internet navigation to more advanced topics like online security and digital communication tools.

Are there any age restrictions for joining?

No, there are no age restrictions. Our services are specifically tailored for seniors, but anyone interested in learning about technology is welcome to join.

What if I miss a class?

We provide recordings of our sessions, and you can always catch up at your convenience. Plus, additional support is available through our website and customer service team.
